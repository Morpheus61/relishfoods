About Us

A New Paradigm in
Clam Processing

Relish Hao Hao Chi Foods is pioneering sustainable, technology-driven clam processing from the heart of Kerala's Vembanad backwaters in Alappuzha — one of the world's richest sources of freshwater clams.

We are building an integrated operation that spans the full lifecycle: from our own hatchery and community-managed grow-out, through advanced depuration and automated processing, to export-ready frozen and pasteurized products.

Why Relish

Key Differentiators

What sets us apart in the global clam processing industry.

ESG Impact

Integrated clam hatchery ensuring sustainable supply without depleting natural populations. A truly circular economy approach.

Community Engagement

Grow-out and harvest managed by local fishermen communities, providing livelihoods while maintaining traditional knowledge.

Waterfront Dock

Direct waterfront access for raw-material off-loading, minimizing transit time and ensuring maximum freshness of live clams.

Advanced Depuration

State-of-the-art depuration system for purging clams of sand, grit, and contaminants before processing — ensuring purity.

Automated Processing

End-to-end automated processing line delivering higher productivity, superior hygiene, and consistent quality control.

Process Flow Automation

Total quality assurance through digital process flow tracking — every lot traced from purchase order to final shipment.
